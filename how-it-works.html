Expanded Workflow

Rel8tion supports the complete relationship lifecycle — from first property interaction to closing continuity.

A. Smart Sign Auto-Setup

Arrive at the open house, tap the sign, and the system activates instantly. Rel8tion launches the right event flow with no manual setup, no extra programming, and no wasted time.

B. Buyer and Agent Routing

Rel8tion routes buyer flow, represented buyer flow, and buyer-agent flow through cleaner pathing so everyone lands in the right workflow with fewer gaps and less confusion.

C. Proof of Relationship

Capture buyer-agent proof before or at the open house to support represented-buyer protection, reduce steering risk, and create a cleaner record trail when questions arise later.

D. Compliance and Broker Oversight

Rel8tion creates digital records inside structured workflows to support cleaner compliance operations while giving brokers better visibility into how interactions are being documented.

E. Automated Follow-Up

The relationship stays alive after first interaction so agents stay in front of buyers longer and reduce the number of opportunities that fade because of delayed outreach.

F. Financing + Deal Support

Rel8tion provides an instant pre-approval pathway and deal-saver support for hard-to-close situations so buyers and agents can keep momentum going toward the closing table.
